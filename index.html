About our Community Centre

Established in 2010 to cater for the expanding Stepaside community, we are very proud to say that we have grown hand-in-hand with our community to ensure we provide the services and amenities that you need.

Looking for activities?

We have developed a wide and varied range of classes to cater for all age groups!
If you would like to find out more information on any of these classes please check out our Schedule page.
